    The Painted Bride is a very unique venue. It houses a gallery space, an art café, and a black box theatre. Martin volunteered, and worked at the Painted Bride during, and after college. It remains his favorite place in Philadelphia. You can even read a love note that Martin wrote to the Painted Bride to learn more about his long-term love affair with this very special place. As you can imagine, we are so excited to celebrate our wedding here with our family and friends!

    The Painted Bride Art Center is conveniently located in historic Old City Philadelphia and just a fifteen-minute walk from the recommended hotels.
